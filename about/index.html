About Horizon Institute of Technology & Science

For more than three decades we have empowered inquisitive minds to innovate, lead, and transform communities through science, technology, and design.

Our Vision

To be a global benchmark for interdisciplinary learning that equips graduates with the agility, empathy, and ingenuity to advance humanity.

Our Mission

We cultivate collaborative ecosystems that merge academic rigor with experiential learning, fostering ethical leaders who solve complex global challenges.

Core Values

Innovation, inclusion, integrity, sustainability, and service anchor every academic initiative, industry partnership, and outreach program.

A Legacy of Pioneering Education

Milestones that mark our commitment to academic excellence, societal impact, and inclusive growth.

1992 — Founding Year

Established by a consortium of industry leaders and academicians to close the skill gap in emerging technologies.

2005 — Global Alliances

Signed strategic partnerships with universities in Europe and Asia, launching dual-degree pathways and exchange programs.

2014 — Innovation District

Unveiled a 40-acre research park enabling startups, labs, and Fortune 500 partners to co-create transformative solutions.

2023 — Sustainability Agenda

Achieved carbon-neutral campus operations through green buildings, solar grids, and community-led conservation initiatives.

Recognitions & Impact

Leading rankings, research accolades, and social contributions that reflect our relentless pursuit of excellence.

QS Asia Top 100

Consistently ranked among the top 100 Asian institutions for engineering and technology.

150+ Patents Filed

Faculty and student teams collaborate with industry to patent breakthrough solutions annually.

Community Outreach

Over 120,000 lives impacted through health camps, STEM schools, and social innovation projects.
